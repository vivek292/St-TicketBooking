FACILITY
  • GSRTC (Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation) was formed on 1st May 1960 by the state government of Gujarat. GSRTC made an immense impact on transportation with its three-level Maintenance and repair facilities at three levels-125 depot workshops, 16 divisional workshops, and a central workshop. and has a fleet of over 9,720 buses; including Multi-axle Volvo, Sleeper and Semi-Sleeper buses in the A/C and Non-A/C Category as well.
  • GSRTC buses operate on all the major routes in numerous states including Gujarati, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi. GSRTC maintains a good reputation with their punctuality, staff behaviour, and hygiene. Some important routes provided by GSRTC operators include Ahmedabad to Dwarka, Ahmedabad to Mata na madh, Ahmedabad to Patan, Baroda to Bhavnagar, Baroda to Diu, Surat to Nagpur, Rajkot to Ambaji, Morbi New to Dahod, Rajkot to Porbandar, Deesa to Ahmedabad CBS, to name a few!
